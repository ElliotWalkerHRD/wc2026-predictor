Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

This is a private predictions game for the 2026 FIFA World Cup, run for a closed group of invited players. It is not a commercial service.

What data we collect

When you sign up we collect:

  • Your email address (used to log in; never shared with other players)
  • Your display name and avatar colour (visible to all players)
  • Your predictions and scores (visible to all players)
  • A timestamp recording when you agreed to this policy

We also store standard technical data such as login timestamps generated by our authentication provider (Supabase).

How your data is used

Your display name, predictions, and scores are shown to all other players on the leaderboard and match pages — this is the core function of the game. Your email address is only used for authentication and is never displayed to other players.

Who can see your data

  • Other players can see your display name, predictions, and scores.
  • Administrators can see all of the above plus your email address.
  • No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Data storage

Data is stored on Supabase, a managed database platform hosted in the EU. Supabase's own privacy policy applies to infrastructure-level data handling.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting the game administrator. Upon deletion, your display name and predictions will be removed from the leaderboard.

Cookies and tracking

We use a session cookie to keep you logged in. No analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking cookies are used.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, players will be notified via the game. The "last updated" date above reflects the current version.

Contact

For any questions about your data, contact the game administrator directly.
