How to play

Scoring

7 pts Exact score — you called both numbers right
5 pts Correct result — right winner or draw, wrong score
0 pts Wrong result
4–4 No prediction submitted — scored as 4-4 by default

The Final is worth more: 10 pts exact · 7 pts correct result.

Rounds 1 & 2 use custom questions judged by the commissioner with their own point values.

The 8 Rounds

  1. Pre-Tournament — trivia & tournament predictions, commissioner-judged
  2. Group Winners — pick the teams topping each group, commissioner-judged
  3. Group Stage — predict all 72 match scores before the tournament begins
  4. Round of 32 — 16 knockout matches
  5. Round of 16 — 8 matches
  6. Quarter Finals — 4 matches
  7. Semi Finals — 2 matches
  8. The Final — 1 match; highest points available

Each round locks before its first kick-off. Picks can’t be changed after the deadline.
